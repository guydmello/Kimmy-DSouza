About Me

Kimmy D’Souza is a performer from Toronto, Ontario. She was a competitive dancer for many years and is in her third year of the Musical Theatre Performance Program at St. Lawrence College in Brockville, Ontario. She attended Father John Redmond C.S.S & R.A.P where she completed a Dance major in Ballet and Modern/Lyrical style of dance. When Kimmy isn’t dancing she is pursuing her love for music. She enjoys playing the piano, and singing out her favorite songs. Her past credits include Mungojerrie in Cats, Molly in Annie, Little Red in Into The Woods, Chip in Beauty and The Beast, Dorothy in The Wizard of Oz, a Player in St. Lawrence College’s production on Pippin and Ensemble in Tweed & Co’s Production of Mamma Mia. Performing has always been a passion for Kimmy since a young age and if her younger self could see her today, she’d be so proud she was pursuing her dreams.
